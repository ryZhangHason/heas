    "NSGA-II as reference implementation. Section 3.2 adopts NSGA-II as the
     default multi-objective evolutionary algorithm. This choice reflects
     three criteria:
     (1) established performance on policy-search benchmarks in the
         simulation-optimization literature;

     and HEAS's agnostic interface enables this flexibility without code
     changes."
