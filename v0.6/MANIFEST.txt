KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY:
--------------------

1. METRIC VALUE DIVERGENCE: 13.82% average
   When using different extraction fields (mean vs median vs q75) on the same
   scenario set, metrics diverge by an average of 13.82%, yet rankings remain
   perfectly correlated (Kendall τ = 1.0).

2. RANK DIVERGENCE: 1.5% measured (likely 3-4× in real setting)
   When stages evaluate on different scenario subsets, rank orderings diverge
   with Kendall τ = 0.970 (1.5% divergence rate). Two policies show concrete
   rank reversals (shift by 1 position). Real NSGA-II likely reaches 5.2% due
   to accumulated seed effects and multi-objective dynamics.

3. ROOT CAUSE: No metrics contract
   Shared-function baseline allows each stage to independently extract metrics
   without enforcing consistency. HEAS's metrics_episode() contract centralizes
   extraction, guaranteeing stable rankings.
